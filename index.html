As parents of young children, we spent two years brainstorming, developing, testing, and perfecting our safety lock design. Most versatile safety lock ever

Our ground-breaking safety lock design uses the ubiquitous side-release buckle with a locking mechanism and a flexible fabric strap to prevent access to almost any container in your home.

The side-release design requires more pinch strength than is typical in a toddler. For extra strong or clever children, the buckle can lock so that not even an adult can release the latch.

These safety locks use reliable 3M© adhesive, so no mounting hardware is required. The adhesive has been tested to 90 lbs pull strength but comes off easily without leaving marks.
